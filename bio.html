Hey There! We are Steve & Tracy

We spent a great week touring around Phuket and ended up staying in Kata about an hour south of Patong. The hotel was very nice and close to the beach, where you can rent some kayaks or stand up boards. You can even get some surfing in on a good day. The beach has plenty of places to rent equipment.

Kata was a great location with lots of restaurants and bars to enjoy a night out. Getting around was a little crazy as the roads are very narrow and busy. The best way to get somewhere would be to use a Tuk-Tuk which is a very cheap way to travel.

As for activities, we found that booking from the hotel was the cheapest and safest way to go, as there are some scams tours. The ones we enjoyed the most were The Big Buddhah, The Elephant Sanctuary, Kata beach, Simon Cabaret Show and of course crazy Patong.

One thing that you must do is reserve a boat tour around the islands. I swear you haven't seen anything like it. The tour we took lasted the whole day and was by far the best part of the trip. We visited islands like: Khao Phing Kan (better known as "James Bond Island"). Also along the trip they take you kayaking and snorkelling in the clearest water you have ever seen. We also stopped on another island to have a buffet and do a little shopping.

The absolute craziest part of the trip was our night out in Patong, where you guessed it "Ping Pong Show". This was by far the weirdest thing we have seen. The main strip is full of bars and strip clubs. If you happen to be close by you should for sure go and check out the nightlife. You will have some crazy stories to tell!!

Thanks!
Steve & Tracy
